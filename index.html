Zeyu Jia

 

I am a senior undergraduate in School of Mathematical Science at Peking University under the supervision of Prof. Zaiwen Wen. I also spent wonderful time working with Prof. Yinyu Ye, Prof. Mengdi Wang and Prof. Lin Yang.

I am interested in reinforcement learning theory, especially in developing provable efficient algorithms for reinforcement learning. I also work on topics in optimization and machine learning, both theory and application.
